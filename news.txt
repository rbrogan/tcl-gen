1.18.0 revision

For this release we have a handful of Date and Time commands which may be
useful for scheduling applications (among others).

LastMonth
     Get the last month.

LastYear
     Get the year before this one.

NextMonth
     Get the next month.

NextYear
     Get the year after this one.

ThisMonth
     Get the current month.

ThisYear
     Get the current year.
