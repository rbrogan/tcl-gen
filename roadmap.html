Gen
Roadmap
1.10
2015.11.16

For the next release, we will do another handful of datetime commands. As far as features go, we may introduce some namespace-related elements as part of a versioning strategy and perhaps may tweak the Partial Loading feature a bit, particularly support for detecting the version of Tcl used and the versions of packages.

At this point, we will most likely go on for a time with monthly releases which include both a modest assortment of commands to keep things moving, as well as infrastructure development.


Want to receive notifications about changes to Gen? You can subscribe to the announcements mailing list by sending an email to gen-announce-subscribe@robertbrogan.com. (No need for anything in subject or message body.) You will get an email every time we have a new release.

Want to see what other features that may be coming? Check out the wishlist.

Do you have a special request for something? You can use the feedback page and let us know. It never hurts to ask!
