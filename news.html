2016.03.21
1.13.0 release

For this revision we have a few simple email convenience commands for use in automated sending and checking of (plaintext) email.

GetEmailUsingImap4

Log onto server with IMAP4, get messages, close connection.

GetEmailUsingPop3

Log onto server with POP3, get messages, close connection.

SendEmail

Send an email to given address, with given subject and body.
